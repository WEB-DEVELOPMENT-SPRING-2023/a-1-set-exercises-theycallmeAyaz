Recipe for Chicken Biryani

Chicken biryani is a popular South Asian dish made with basmati rice, chicken, and spices like cumin, coriander, turmeric,and red chili powder.
The dish is known for its unique flavor achieved through layering the rice and chicken curry and slow-cooking over low heat.It's ussualy served with raita,
a refreshing side dish made with yogurt,grated cucumber, onions,and coriander leaves, which balances out the spiciness of the biryani.
Together,they make a satisfying meal enjoyed by many.

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups Basmati rice, washed and soaked in water for 30 minutes
  • 500 grams chicken, cut into small pieces
  • 2 onions, sliced
  • 2 tomatoes, chopped
  • 4 green chillies, slit
  • 1 tablespoon ginger-garlic paste
  • 1/2 cup yogurt
  • 1/4 cup chopped mint leaves
  • 1/4 cup chopped coriander leaves
  • 1 teaspoon cumin seeds
  • 1 teaspoon coriander powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon turmeric powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon red chilli powder
  • 1/2 teaspoon garam masala powder
  • 2-3 tablespoons oil or ghee
  • Salt to taste

Method:

  1. Heat oil or ghee in a large pan and add cumin seeds. When the seeds start to crackle, add sliced onions and fry till they turn golden brown.
  2. Add ginger-garlic paste and fry for 1-2 minutes. Then add chopped tomatoes and green chillies and fry for another 2-3 minutes.
  3. Add chicken pieces and fry for 5-6 minutes, or until the chicken turns brown.
  4. Add coriander powder, turmeric powder, red chilli powder, and salt to taste.Mix well and cook for 2-3 minutes.
  5. Add yogurt, mint leaves, and coriander leaves. Mix well and cook for another 5-6 minutes.
  6. In a separate pot, boil water and add soaked Basmati rice. Cook the rice until it is 70-80% done.
  7. Drain the water from the rice and spread half of the rice in the bottom of a large pot or pressure cooker. Layer the chicken curry on top of the rice.
  8. Add the remaining rice on top of the chicken curry.
  9. Sprinkle garam masala powder on top of the rice.
  10. Cover the pot with a tight-fitting lid and cook on low heat for 20-25 minutes, or until the rice is fully cooked.
  11. Serve the biryani hot

Additionally:

  1. To make the raita, mix together yogurt, grated cucumber, chopped onion, coriander leaves, and salt to taste.
  2. keep this mixture(raita) in the fridge for 30 minutes
  3. Serve the biryani hot with raita on the side.
